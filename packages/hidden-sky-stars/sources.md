# Sources — Hidden Sky / Star Visibility

- Ptolemy, *Almagest*, Book VII–VIII
- Hipparchus Star Catalog (c. 129 BCE)
- Galileo Galilei, *Sidereus Nuncius* (1610)

## Modern Scientific Sources

- Falchi et al., “The New World Atlas of Artificial Night Sky Brightness” (2016)
- Kyba et al., “Artificially Lit Surface of Earth at Night Increasing in Radiance” (2017)

## Historical Accounts

- Pliny the Elder, *Natural History*
- Pre-industrial navigation logs (17th–18th century)

## Notes

- All speculative interpretations are derived from gaps or inconsistencies in
  documented observations and are not asserted as fact.
